# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who interacts with this project. All contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to behave in a professional and courteous manner.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Treat othersâ€™ ideas, code, and feedback with fairness
- Provide constructive feedback without personal attacks
- Keep discussions focused on the project and technical issues
- Respect diversity in experience, background, and opinions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Sharing private information without consent
- Disruptive or aggressive behavior that hinders collaboration
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe or hostile environment

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please raise an issue in the gituhub repo. Reports will be reviewed confidentially, and appropriate action will be taken to resolve the issue.

## Enforcement

The project maintainers reserve the right to take any action deemed necessary to maintain a positive and safe environment, including removing contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.

## Commitment to Improvement

We encourage all contributors to help uphold this Code of Conduct and suggest improvements to make our community more inclusive and respectful.
